AMUN-I

APS Army Public School & College, Dhamial successfully hosted its first-ever Model United Nations Conference (AMUN) on December 23rd at Aviation Base, Dhamial. This milestone event was organized with the kind permission and continuous support of Commander 101, whose guidance made it possible to conduct the conference on such a large scale. The administration and students sincerely appreciated his support, which turned this landmark initiative into a success.
