About APSACS DHAMIAL

Army Public School & College Dhamial, established in 1968, is a reputable educational institution with a strong tradition of academic excellence and discipline. Formerly known as Army Aviation Public School and College Dhamial, the institution has a long history of providing quality education. In February 2020, it was formally affiliated with the Army Public Schools & Colleges System (APSACS). The school is also affiliated with the Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE) and offers SSC-I and SSC-II at the school level, along with HSSC-I and HSSC-II programs at the college level. APS Dhamial operates through separate Boys and Girls branches, ensuring a focused and supportive learning environment. The institution remains committed to holistic development, character building, and preparing students to meet the academic and professional challenges of the future.

College

ICS

1st Year (HSSC-I)

Students study core science subjects including Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Compulsory subjects are English, Urdu, Islamic Studies, and Tarjuma-tul-Quran.

2nd Year (HSSC-II)

Core science subjects remain Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Other compulsory subjects include English, Urdu, Islamic Studies, and Pakistan Studies.
